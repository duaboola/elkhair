FOUNDER

Peter Elkhair, the visionary founder of a leading hospitality consultancy, is making waves in the industry with his two distinct brands—Kryo Lounge and Restaurant, and a premium tobacco and hookah outlet. With a deep-rooted passion for redefining leisure experiences, Peter has established himself as an entrepreneurial force.

In the realm of hospitality, Peter's consultancy provides invaluable expertise to businesses aiming to enhance their offerings. His commitment to excellence is further evident in the success of Kryo Lounge and Restaurant, where avant-garde design meets curated culinary experiences, creating an ambiance that elevates each visit into a memorable occasion.

Expanding his portfolio, Peter introduced a premium tobacco and hookah outlet, setting a new standard for leisure. Known for its exceptional selection of top-notch tobacco products and exquisite hookah flavors, this establishment has swiftly become a haven for connoisseurs seeking the epitome of relaxation.

Peter Elkhair's journey is marked by relentless pursuit of excellence, innovation, and an unwavering dedication to delivering unparalleled hospitality experiences. As he continues to shape the industry landscape, each venture under his guidance stands as a testament to his passion for creating exceptional spaces and setting new benchmarks in luxury and leisure.
